Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

SubExpense ("we", "our", or "the app") is a subscription and recurring expense tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and name. If you use Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name and email from those providers.

Subscription data: The subscriptions and recurring expenses you add to the app, including names, amounts, billing dates, and categories. This data is stored securely in our database.

Income data: If you choose to enter your income for insights, this is stored with your profile.

Device information: We may collect basic device info (OS version, device type) for crash reporting and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core app functionality (tracking subscriptions, reminders, insights)
  • To send you push notifications about upcoming payments and trial expirations
  • To generate spending analytics and personalized tips
  • To sync your data across linked devices

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — for authentication and data storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In — for authentication only. We only receive your name and email.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with row-level security — only you can access your own data. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission.

5. Advertising

SubExpense displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising identifiers and cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described in this policy (third-party services above). We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You can delete individual subscriptions at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

SubExpense is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
